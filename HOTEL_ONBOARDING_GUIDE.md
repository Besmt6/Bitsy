# Bitsy Hotel Onboarding Guide
**Get your hotel live in 5 minutes**

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account (1 minute)

1. Go to **getbitsy.ai**
2. Click **"Get Started Free"**
3. Enter:
   - Your email (e.g., `manager@yourhotel.com`)
   - Password (min 6 characters)
   - Hotel name (e.g., `Beach Paradise Resort`)
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. ✅ You're in! Welcome to your dashboard.

---

## Step 2: Configure Hotel Settings (2 minutes)

1. Click **"Settings"** in sidebar
2. Fill in basic info:
   - **Hotel Name**: Your property name
   - **Contact Phone**: For guest inquiries
   - **Contact Email**: Where booking confirmations go
   - **Logo URL**: Your hotel logo (or upload via Photo Uploader)
   - **Hotel Gallery**: Upload up to 8 photos showcasing your property

3. **Optional - Telegram Notifications**:
   - Get instant booking alerts on Telegram
   - Create bot: Message @BotFather on Telegram → `/newbot`
   - Copy Bot Token and Chat ID → Paste in settings

4. Click **"Save Settings"**
5. ✅ Hotel profile complete!

---

## Step 3: Add Your Rooms (1 minute)

1. Click **"Rooms"** in sidebar
2. Click **"Add Room"** button
3. For each room type, enter:
   - **Room Type**: e.g., "Deluxe Suite", "Standard Double"
   - **Description**: "Ocean view with king bed" (optional)
   - **Rate per Night**: e.g., `199` (in USD)
   - **Available Count**: How many rooms of this type (e.g., `5`)
   - **Room Photos**: Upload up to 6 photos
4. Click **"Create"**
5. Repeat for all room types
6. ✅ Rooms ready for booking!

---

## Step 4: Add Crypto Wallet Addresses (1 minute)

1. Click **"Wallets"** in sidebar
2. Add your wallet addresses for each blockchain:
   - **Bitcoin** (BTC): e.g., `1A1zP1eP5QGefi2DMPTfTL5SLmv7DivfNa`
   - **Ethereum** (ETH, USDC, USDT): e.g., `0x742d35Cc6634C0532925a3b844Bc9e7595f0bEb1`
   - **Polygon** (MATIC, USDC): e.g., `0x742d35Cc6634C0532925a3b844Bc9e7595f0bEb1`
   - **Base** (ETH, USDC): e.g., `0x742d35Cc6634C0532925a3b844Bc9e7595f0bEb1`
   - **Arbitrum** (ETH): e.g., `0x742d35Cc6634C0532925a3b844Bc9e7595f0bEb1`
   - **Optimism** (ETH): e.g., `0x742d35Cc6634C0532925a3b844Bc9e7595f0bEb1`

**Note**: For EVM chains (Ethereum, Polygon, Base, Arbitrum, Optimism), you can use the SAME address.

3. Click **"Save Wallets"**
4. ✅ Ready to accept crypto payments!

**Important**: Payments go DIRECTLY to your wallets. Bitsy never holds your funds.

---

## Step 5: Embed Widget on Your Website (<1 minute)

1. Click **"Widget"** in sidebar
2. Copy the embed code (looks like):
   ```html
   <script 
     src="https://getbitsy.ai/widget/bitsy-widget.js" 
     data-hotel-id="YOUR_HOTEL_ID"
   ></script>
   ```
3. Paste this code **before the `</body>` tag** on your website
4. Works with:
   - ✅ WordPress (Appearance → Theme Editor → footer.php)
   - ✅ Wix (Settings → Custom Code → Footer)
   - ✅ Squarespace (Settings → Advanced → Code Injection)
   - ✅ Shopify (Themes → Edit Code → theme.liquid)
   - ✅ Any HTML website

5. Save and publish
6. ✅ Bitsy chat button appears on your site!

---

3. Try asking: "What rooms are available?"
4. AI should respond with your room options and prices
5. Test a booking flow (optional - won't charge you)
6. ✅ Widget is live!

## FAQ

**Q: How do guests pay?**  
A: Two ways:
1. **MetaMask**: Connect wallet → Select chain → Send payment (instant verification)
2. **QR Code**: Scan QR with mobile wallet → Send payment → Confirm with hotel

**Q: When do I get paid?**  
A: Instantly! Payments go directly to your wallet addresses. Bitsy never holds funds.

**Q: What if a guest asks a question the AI can't answer?**  
A: The AI is trained on your room data. For complex questions, it will provide your contact email/phone.

**Q: Can I customize the widget appearance?**  
A: Currently, widget uses your brand colors from settings. White-label customization available in Enterprise plan ($299/month).

**Q: How does MCP/AI discovery work?**  
A: When travelers ask "Find me hotels that accept crypto", ChatGPT/Claude queries Bitsy's MCP endpoint. If you match their criteria (location, price, blockchain), your hotel appears in results automatically.

**Q: Do I need crypto wallets for all 6 chains?**  
A: No! Add only the blockchains you want to accept. Most hotels start with Ethereum (ETH, USDC, USDT).

**Q: What if I don't have a crypto wallet?**  
A: Create one:
- **Coinbase Wallet**: coinbase.com/wallet (easiest)
- **MetaMask**: metamask.io (most popular)
- **Hardware wallet**: Ledger or Trezor (most secure)
